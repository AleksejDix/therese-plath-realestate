Residential property at GOLDSBOROUGH on a m2

$380,000 ( Neg )

22 - 24, Windlass Street, GOLDSBOROUGH 3 1 4

Land area: m2

Property ID CSS4511741

Situated in the beautiful hills of Goldsborough Valley, is where you will find this delightful home that is built on a completely fenced 4,000m2 block, and offers enough space for all family members to enjoy. There is a large open plan air conditioned living area, spacious kitchen with island bench, dishwasher, large pantry and a rear patio to relax and enjoy the views. All bedrooms are a great size, they come with split system air conditioning and builtins, while the main enjoys a walk through bathroom. There is a 9m x 6m powered shed with 9m x 2m awning to the front. It comes with fans, insulation and two roller doors. Easy access to the shed is provided through a second set of double gates at the front of the property. Positioned to one side of the shed is a 7,000lt water tank with electric pump, which is connected to four taps that have been located throughout the property for convenience. This will also come in handy when washing down the car or boat. There are pop up sprinklers and garden irrigation in place surrounding the house, with mature trees adding to privacy from the road front. Fishing and boating for the kids is located close by, and the school bus stops at the top of the street.
