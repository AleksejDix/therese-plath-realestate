Property ID CSS9539965

Therese Plath is excited to present this outstanding acreage property offering an enviable lifestyle and all within only 5 minutes to schools, shopping and sporting facilities in the family friendly township of Gordonvale and just 20 minutes easy drive to Cairns City and the famous Esplanade. This expansive family home is a superb example of unrestricted living with lifestyle and space to spare. Set on 4,633 square metres in one of Cairns' South's most desirable suburbs, it caters to contemporary family living without compromise. Step past the gated entry to the fully fenced yard to be greeted by a proud residence that incorporates a generous floor plan, offering multiple living spaces and the flexibility to accommodate families of all sizes. Formal and casual areas blend together to create a warm and welcoming ambience with neutral tones, natural light and cooling breezes. Established and private with a clever blend of features and practical family friendly design, this fully air conditioned home has an easy casual Elegance. There are four generously proportioned bedrooms throughout the home allowing privacy and ample space for the entire family, with an en-suite to the master, and a separate study. Practical elements and appointments have not been overlooked with kitchen featuring granite bench tops, stainless steel appliances and gas cook top. The central position is ideal for serving meals to the adjoining dining area, family room and alfresco entertaining area overlooking the sparkling in-ground pool. Two huge double lock up sheds provide plenty of room for the tradie or car/boat enthusiast with 5 car lock up garages plus extra under cover area for all your BIG toys. Shady trees and gardens attract the kingfishers, butterflies and resident kookaburras. The underground irrigation bore keeps the lawns lush and green all year round and ensures the established fruit trees are regularly watered. Your children have rolling grassed lawns to run around and kick the footy in the safely fenced environment. There is even room for a horse! We welcome your interest and inspection.
