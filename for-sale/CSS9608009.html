Land area: 710m2

Therese Plath and Patrick Kenny present this great family home located 15 minutes from Cairns City Centre and just 5 minutes to schools and shopping nearby.

This property is impeccably presented, epitomising a myriad of family friendly features. The 5kw solar system provides savings all year round, splilt system air conditioning throughout all rooms of the home and a low maintenance above ground pool that's great for an afternoon dip while enjoying the gardens makes this home complete.

For the budding chef of the home this impressive kitchen has quality fittings throughout with banks of cupboards, ample bench space and gas cook top. It is an entertainers dream with easy access to the living areas and bi-fold windows to the huge undercover tiled alfresco entertaining area.

The master bedroom is large and has an en-suite with the main bathroom servicing the other two generous bedrooms, both with built-ins and a separate toilet, plunge bath/shower and powder room.

The gardens are low maintenance featuring a rock retaining wall and well established tropical gardens with lock up lawn locker shed, bird avery and green houses. The property has secure child and pet friendly fencing and the level block has access through the double remote car accommodation.

An ideal lifestyle choice for those seeking low maintenance living in a prime position just minutes to the Cairns City and Esplanade and with a number of parks, walking tracks, schools, sporting facilities and Mount Sheridan Shopping Plaza.

Make the most of this family home - If you value difference, quality and an easy lifestyle. We look forward to showing you this fine property at your convenience.
