Residential property at GORDONVALE on a 170m2

Price Upon Application

432 Russell Road, GORDONVALE 4 4 6

Land area: 170m2

Property ID: CSS9540141

Therese Plath is proud to present approximately 170 acres of prime agricultural land near Gordonvale here in Cairns. As you know these types of properties are far and few between.

Designed for family living with a semi-rural lifestyle in mind where you can raise your children with a sense of freedom. This family owned operation have been farming for many years and is well known for their quality produce and good farming practices.

This is a unique opportunity to purchase a successful business as a going concern. Currently being run as a cane farm with a massive 500m2 executive family home surrounded by a manicured garden setting and valley views to the Pyramid where you feel you can reach out and touch it.

The well appointed and thoughtfully designed family home is all about living and entertaining with a versatile floor plan offering space with an indoor / outdoor blend.

This home blends sumptuous lines with quality materials that feature through the entire design. Expansive living areas are enhanced by four oversized bedrooms which are positioned near two other bathrooms, with is tiled floor to ceiling has granite benches and plunge bath.

The parents retreat is away from the other bedrooms comprising of walk in robe, private en-suite, tiled to ceiling, spa, granite bench, double vanities and double shower.

The relaxed ambience is evident when you walk from the main open plan living, dining and kitchen area onto the adjoining massive tiled entertaining patio which overlooks the surrounding mountains just perfect for weekend celebrations.

The kitchen is modern and boasts granite bench tops, Blackwood timber cabinetry, plenty of bench and preparation areas with easy reach storage and quality stainless steel appliances - This gourmet space will be a joy to the executive chef in the family.

Good river flats for a few head of cattle this is without doubt the best of the very best available with 11 paddocks in total.

The massive shed has solar power panels which also run the home, electric gantries has 3 phase power and it's very own bathroom and extra storage facilities.

The current owners have over the last couple of years completed extensive programs on improving the management and have continued to improve their productivity and yields. The cropping has been selectively planted in smaller blocks at different time intervals for ease of management.

The property boundaries the river for a substantial distance and water supply is augmented by a permanent creek offering assurance of good water, highly productive soil types, high yields and great location. There is also town water and bore for washing of equipment.
