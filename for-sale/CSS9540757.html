Therese Plath is thrilled to release this stunning residence located in one of Cairns' most desirable suburbs only 4kms to Cairns City Centre and the popular Cairns Esplanade and yet only 10 minutes to the airport and less travelling to schools and every convenience nearby.

Ideal for the consummate entertainer this meticulously constructed home was architecturally designed and offers a relaxed yet elegant atmosphere with all the living areas capturing the sparkles of the city perfect for entertaining.

Featuring high ceilings and extravagant banks of glass that demonstrate a light filled interior and the promise of comfort in the open plan design.

Fully air conditioned and family friendly the floor plan encompasses the generous master with en-suite and walk in robe, with office / media, plus 3 other bedrooms, main bathroom up 3 stairs on the higher level.

The modern kitchen of granite is perfect to serve you delightful Sunday brunch on the outside terrace, formal dinner parties or casual entertaining on the deck.

Lots of storage and room for your craft work in the spacious laundry also including 3 secure side by side lock up garaging all on remote control and room for Dad to tinker.

You will love the ambience created with all the hard work completed as you discover the beautiful established mini botanical tropical gardens of variety and colour.

This welcoming residence is all about lifestyle waking to a fresh sunrise every morning or relaxing in the private courtyard with almost every need at your doorstop.

Situated on this generous 800m of impressive gardens this winning home is positioned to capture year round cooling breezes and has its own plantation style identity and charm, privacy and magic views.
